Itinerary

Daily plans

Paris base: 7 Avenue Stephen Pichon, Bâtiment B, rez de chaussée, 75013.

Day 1 — Tuesday 2 Dec
  • 11:05 Land CDG
  • RER B → Denfert-Rochereau → M6 Place d’Italie
  • Check in at 7 Avenue Stephen Pichon, Bât. B
  • Chinatown stroll and lunch near Tang Frères or Bep Viet
  • Walk or metro to Bastille, enter Coulée Verte
  • Late afternoon drink at Le Train Bleu (Gare de Lyon)
  • Dinner at Afrik’N’Fusion (13th)
  • Walk back home HQ
Day 2 — Wednesday 3 Dec
  • Sainte-Chapelle and Conciergerie in the morning
  • Walk by Notre-Dame (exterior) and Pont des Arts
  • Jardin du Luxembourg stroll
  • Lunch at Huîtrerie Régis (oysters)
  • Afternoon at Musée d’Orsay
  • Dinner at Chez Gladines (13th)
Day 3 — Thursday 4 Dec
  • Focused Louvre visit in 19th-century rooms
  • Walk through Tuileries and Christmas market if open
  • Lunch at Chez Alain Miam Miam
  • Walk toward Passy and Maison de Balzac
  • Late afternoon at Trocadéro, Eiffel views
  • Dinner at Le Temps des Cerises (Butte-aux-Cailles)
Day 4 — Friday 5 Dec (Rouen)
  • Métro to Saint-Lazare for 08:14 train to Rouen
  • Old town and Rouen Cathedral, Gros Horloge
  • Lunch at Vegan & Cie or nearby brasserie
  • Musée Flaubert et d’Histoire de la Médecine
  • Cimetière monumental and grave of Flaubert
  • Optional Croisset Pavilion, taxi back to station
  • Train back to Paris and dinner near Saint-Lazare
Day 5 — Saturday 6 Dec
  • Belleville walk and Dora Bruder Promenade
  • Montmartre and Sacré-Cœur
  • Père-Lachaise Cemetery
  • Dinner with aligot or raclette (Pain Vin Fromages or similar)
Day 6 — Sunday 7 Dec
  • Parc Montsouris loop from home HQ
  • Covered passages and central Paris wandering
  • Tuileries Christmas Market and nearby sights
  • Galeries Lafayette for lights and dome
  • Carrefour Italie 2 grocery stop
  • Dinner at Darkoum Cantine Marocaine (Rue Daguerre)
Day 7 — Monday 8 Dec (Return)
  • Early departure from apartment
  • Métro Place d’Italie → RER B to CDG
  • Check in and security at CDG before 10:30 flight
  • DTW layover and evening arrival in Appleton
